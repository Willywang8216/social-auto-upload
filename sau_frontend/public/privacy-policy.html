SocialUpload Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-03

This Privacy Policy explains how SocialUpload handles media assets, connected account data, and operational information when users manage and publish social content through the service.

1. Information we process

Depending on how SocialUpload is used, the service may process uploaded media files, post captions, scheduling metadata, connected account identifiers, cookies, API access tokens, and platform-specific publishing settings.

2. How we use information

We use this information to provide account connection, content preparation, scheduling, publishing, media storage, and operational logging features.

3. Third-party platforms

When you connect a third-party platform such as TikTok, SocialUpload may transmit content and account-related metadata to that platform strictly for the features you authorize, such as login, content publishing, or media transfer.

4. Data retention

Uploaded files, generated drafts, cookies, and platform configuration data may be retained for operational purposes until removed by the user or administrator, subject to system configuration and backup policies.

5. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect stored data, including access controls and, where configured, encryption for stored account session files. No security measure can guarantee absolute protection.

6. User responsibility

You are responsible for ensuring that you have the right to upload, store, publish, and process the content and account data you provide to SocialUpload.

7. Children’s privacy

SocialUpload is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to use connected social platforms without authorization.

8. Policy updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the service after an update indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the operator through the official SocialUpload contact channel.
